# 🚀 Rohith Builds: Platform Growth & Monetization Blueprint

This document outlines the step-by-step roadmap to launch, market, grow, and monetize **Rohith Builds** using performance-based acquisition, affiliate marketing, and micro-SaaS tools.

---

## 🌐 Phase 1: Brand & Domain Launch Checklist

Before launching any marketing campaigns, you must connect a custom domain to make the platform look professional and build brand trust.

### 1. Purchase a Domain
Buy a developer-focused domain (e.g., `rohithbuilds.com`, `rohithbuilds.dev`, or `rohithbuilds.co`) from a registrar like Namecheap or Google Domains.

### 2. Configure DNS on Render (Free)
1. Go to your **Render Dashboard** → Select your Web Service.
2. Click **Settings** → Scroll down to **Custom Domains** → Click **Add Custom Domain**.
3. Enter your domain (e.g., `rohithbuilds.com`) and click **Save**.
4. Log into your domain registrar and add these two DNS records:
   * **CNAME Record:** Host: `www` | Value: `your-subdomain.onrender.com`
   * **A Record:** Host: `@` (Root) | Value: `75.2.60.5` (Or the IP address Render provides)
5. Wait 5-10 minutes. Render will automatically issue a free SSL certificate (`HTTPS`), and your site will be live on your custom domain!

## 📈 Phase 2: Performance-Based Internship Program

To gain your first 500+ active users without paying cash upfront, hire student marketing interns on a performance-based (milestone) stipend.

### 1. The Payout Structure
Students get paid in tiers as they deliver verified users:
*   **Milestone 1:** 100 active learners reached ➡️ Pay **₹1,500 (~$20)**
*   **Milestone 2:** 200 active learners reached ➡️ Pay **₹1,500 (~$20)**
*   **Milestone 3:** 300 active learners reached ➡️ Pay **₹1,500 (~$20)**
*   **Milestone 4:** 500 active learners reached ➡️ Pay **₹3,000 (~$40) Bonus**
*   *Total cost for 500 active users: ₹7,500 (~$100)*

### 🛡️ The "Active Learner" Spam-Protection Rule
To prevent interns from creating fake accounts or registering friends who never use the site, a user only counts toward milestones if they:
1. **Verify their email address** (prevents bots/fake emails).
2. **Earn at least 100 XP** (proves they logged in, opened the course, and completed Day 1).

### 📍 Where to Hire Candidates
*   **Internshala:** Post a "Sales & Marketing Internship" with a performance-based stipend.
*   **LinkedIn/Twitter:** Post an internship opening targeting Computer Science university students.
*   **University Groups:** Share in coding clubs and WhatsApp/Discord tech groups.

## 💸 Phase 3: Paid Courses & Affiliate Program

Once you build an active user base of 500+ members, launch premium paywalled courses and turn your top students into a commission-based sales team.

### 1. The Affiliate Model
*   Give each student a unique referral link (e.g., `rohithbuilds.com/course/saas-flask?ref=student_id`).
*   Offer them a **30% commission** on every premium course sale they refer.
*   If your premium course costs **$20**:
    *   The student marketer earns **$6 (₹500)** per sale.
    *   You keep **$14 (₹1,100)** as profit.

### 2. Why this is self-sustaining:
You have **zero upfront marketing costs**. Your students actively write blogs, post on LinkedIn, and share links in class groups to make money for themselves, which passively grows your website.

## 💻 Phase 4: Platform Monetization Engine

Here are the 4 ways **Rohith Builds** will generate revenue:

### 1. Premium Course Sales (LMS)
Keep foundational 7-day courses (like the AI Agent course) free to build your email list and user base, then upsell advanced courses (e.g., "Build a Micro-SaaS with Flask & Stripe" for $19–$29).

### 2. Creator Studio SaaS Subscriptions
Integrate your **Creator Studio v2.0 Web Edition** directly into the platform:
*   **Free Tier:** 3 video edits / AI voice cleans per month.
*   **Pro Tier ($9/month):** Unlimited AI denoising (DeepFilterNet3) and editing canvas presets.

### 3. Featured Job Postings
Charge tech companies **$49** to pin featured developer jobs on your built-in Job Board.

### 4. YouTube Sponsorships
As your course traffic grows, tech companies (like database, hosting, or API providers) will pay you **$100–$300** to mention them in your coding videos.
